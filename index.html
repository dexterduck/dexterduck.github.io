My name is Dexter Duckworth. I am a senior studying computer engineering at Mississippi State University. My primary area of interest is robotics with specialization in human-robot interaction and bio-inspired robotics. I have spent the last three years working in the STARS lab at Mississippi State doing research in human-robot interaction and spent a summer doing field robotics research in the CRASAR lab at Texas A & M University.

Most of my lab work has been in Python, specifically RosPy, the Python version of the Robot Operating System. I have also worked extensively in C/C++ both for simple Arduino projects and operating system design. I have some web development experience, including HTML/CSS, Javascript, PHP, and MySQL.

In my free time, I like to cook and play guitar. I have played guitar for about 8 years and I enjoy covering artists such as Dispatch, Mumford and Sons, Frank Sinatra, and Jonathan Coulton. When I have time, I play shows in the Memphis area. I have a dog named Willow who is a blue tick coonhound. Pictures of her can be found here.
